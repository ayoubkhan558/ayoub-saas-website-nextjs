# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small business owners, agency teams, and product founders who need a reliable WordPress, WooCommerce, CMS, or front-end developer. They are evaluating credibility quickly and need proof that the work will be fast, maintainable, and easy to manage after launch.

## Product Purpose

This homepage presents Muhammad Ayoub's portfolio and service offering, then moves visitors toward a clear project inquiry. Success means visitors understand the services, see relevant proof, trust the process, and can contact Ayoub without friction.

## Brand Personality

Precise, practical, and technically confident. The site should feel like a dependable delivery system with enough craft to prove design taste, but it should never bury the message under decorative effects.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS template sections, unreadable dark-gray copy, overanimated terminal decoration, repeated tiny uppercase labels, vague claims without proof, and dense card grids that make every section feel equally important.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the business outcome before the technical mechanism.
- Make proof visible and scannable.
- Keep the technical identity, but prioritize human readability.
- Use motion to clarify arrival and focus, not to compete with content.
- Make every call to action obvious on desktop and mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, touch targets of at least 44px for primary interactions, readable body copy on mobile, and reduced-motion support for all decorative animation.
